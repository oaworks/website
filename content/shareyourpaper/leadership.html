We’re community owned, so we’re asking you to buy in

Founded in 2013 by students frustrated by paywalls, we’ve worked since to help pioneer simple delivery of open access content in repositories, doing that in Interlibrary Loan and browser plugins, and more effective self-archiving workflows with funding from Arcadia Fund, Jisc, the Open Society Foundations, Center for Open Science, and SPARC.
