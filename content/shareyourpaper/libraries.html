Saves Time

  • No permissions checking: we built a system that automates permissions checking.
  • No version checking: we check every file automatically, giving authors instant feedback.
  • No juggling files: authors can easily drag and drop files into your repository, not your inbox.
  • No metadata hunting: we’ll collect openly available metadata for your deposits.
  • No Q&A our simple interface provides authors everything they need to know.

Improve Services

  • Easy deposit = more deposits: by making deposit drag and drop, with instant gratification, expect more deposits!
  • Empowers champions: with simple links directly to deposit that always make work more open.
  • Build Relationships: we’ll notify you in real time when authors need help.
  • Embed deposit anywhere: from your website, to promotion forms, and more.
