Make sharing in your repository the simplest way to increase your author’s impact.

Sharing should be simple. With shareyourpaper.org, we' make sure that deposit into any repository means only dragging and dropping the paper. We’re building a workflow that removes barriers we’ve seen after asking thousands of authors to self-archive, as well as easily upgrades the deposit workflow in thousands of repositories. For libraries, shareyourpaper.org helps you fill your repository by offering the simplest possible deposit workflow for authors, while saving you time and requiring no migrations or upgrades to your current repository.

Saves Time

  • No permissions checking: we built a system that automates permissions checking.
  • No version checking: we check every file automatically, giving authors instant feedback.
  • No juggling files: authors can easily drag and drop files into your repository, not your inbox.
  • No metadata hunting: we’ll collect openly available metadata for your deposits.
  • No Q&A our simple interface provides authors everything they need to know.
