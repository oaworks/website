Integrations

with library systems

Want to provide your patrons more access to research? Integrating Open Access (especially self-archived) content into your Library Discovery System, Link resolver, website, or Interlibrary Loan system backend or form can be ways to do it. For Interlibrary Loan, see here, for websites go here, for everything else, read on!

Our friends at Unpaywall also maintain a fantastic integrations page (e.g for SFX and Primo) and they work great if you have DOIs. If you don't, or also want to make requests for content, read on.

  • Ebsco's Full Text Finder
  • 360 link & Summon/Link 2.0

Can't see an option for your system? Consider making one, depending on your system you might not even need to know how to code or work with your vendor to get something set-up. Otherwise, reach out and we'd be excited to lend a hand.

First, please sign up / sign in to see a customized link that helps us get in touch if things go wrong and understand how the service is being used..

You can use 

https://openaccessbutton.org/?from=integration&id=
followed by a DOI, URL, Title, Citation, PMID or PMC ID given by your system. For more advanced integrations try our free, documented api.
