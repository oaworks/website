Suggested contribution levels

You may support the Open Access Button at any level. Suggested contribution levels are as follows, depending on the Carnegie classification of your institution:

  • Doctoral Universities (R tier): $5000
  • Master's Colleges and Universities (M tier): $2500
  • Baccalaureate Colleges: $1500

For all other pricing, including consortial, and overseas pricing, please contact us for a suggested level.
